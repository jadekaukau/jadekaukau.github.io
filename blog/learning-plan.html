A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

Each week day, I commit to at least 1 hour after work hours of study. This usually becomes much longer, however I find that if my house isn’t in order I will do washing, make dinner, give my cat some attention etc. My weekends are dedicated to study unless I have a prebooked engagement, which means I adjust the week day study sessions to make sure I have heaps of time to complete the work. When things are building up, that’s when I need to cancel social plan and prioritise, calling on my partner for support (they can take care of day to day life thunigs). I also chop my study requirements into clear time slots, making sure I stick to them in order to complete the work on time.
